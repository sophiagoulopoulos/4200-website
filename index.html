Welcome to My Website

I have always been someone who likes to understand how things work. Whether it is technology, businesses, or the way people collaborate, I tend to ask a lot of questions and enjoy figuring things out. That curiosity is what led me to study Computer Science and Business at Northeastern University. I like the analytical side of problem solving, but I am also interested in how ideas turn into real products and how teams work together to make that happen. During college I have tried to pursue opportunities that let me explore both sides. In my technical work, I enjoy building tools that make information easier to understand. While working with engineering teams, I helped develop dashboards that combined financial and labor data so project managers could better track progress and performance. I find it rewarding when technical work has a clear practical purpose and helps people make better decisions. Outside of academics, I work as a Resident Assistant, where I support students in my building and help create an environment where people feel comfortable and connected. The role has taught me a lot about communication and leadership. Music is also a big part of my life. I have always loved singing and being involved in music groups on campus. I also enjoy playing volleyball and tennis when I have time. Overall, I enjoy learning and exploring different interests. I am excited by opportunities that allow me to combine technology, business, and collaboration while continuing to grow.
