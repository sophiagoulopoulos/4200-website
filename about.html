My Hobbies

Outside of school, I enjoy spending my time doing activities that allow me to relax, be creative, and stay active. One of my favorite hobbies is singing. Music has always been an important part of my life because it helps me express myself and unwind when things get stressful. Singing allows me to focus on something creative and enjoyable while also connecting with others who love music. Whether it is practicing songs, listening to new artists, or performing in groups, music has always been something that brings me a lot of happiness.

Another hobby I enjoy is playing sports, especially volleyball and tennis. Sports are a great way for me to stay active and spend time with friends. I like the competitive aspect of sports, but I also enjoy the teamwork and energy that comes from playing with others. Being involved in physical activities helps me stay balanced when school becomes busy, and it gives me something fun to look forward to outside of academic work.

I also enjoy working on creative and technical projects. As someone studying computer science, I like experimenting with building small websites and learning how different technologies work together. Projects like this one help me improve my skills while also allowing me to design something of my own. Overall, my hobbies help me stay creative, active, and curious about learning new things.

Some things i do:

  • Singing and listening to music
  • Playing volleyball
  • Playing tennis
  • Working on technical projects
  • Spending time with friends
